üìñ Step 2: How to Use (The Basics)

The "Always On" Rule

In TeamSpeak, there is no "Join Voice" button. When you enter a channel, you are live. If you need silence, you must manually mute your mic or move to a different room.

Moving Between Channels

To move from the Lobby to a specific game room, double-click the channel name. You will "teleport" there instantly. You can see who is in every room at a glance without having to join first.

The AFK / Away System

Don't disconnect if you're just grabbing a snack. Click the Away (Clock) icon in the top toolbar. This mutes you and your speakers so you don't hear us, and we don't hear you. The server will eventually move you to the AFK/Idle channel automatically if you stop moving your mouse.

Poking Users

If someone is tabbed out, right-click their name and select "Poke Client." This sends a direct alert sound and a popup on their screen. It's the "Discord @mention" but way harder to ignore.
