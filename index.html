üìñ Beginner's Guide: How to Use TS6

If you're coming from Discord, TeamSpeak works a little differently. Here‚Äôs the breakdown:

1. The "Always On" Connection

In TeamSpeak, there is no "Join Voice" button. When you are in a channel, your voice is live. You don't have to click a separate room to start talking; just connect and you're in.

2. Moving Channels (Double-Click)

To move from the Lobby to a game room, simply double-click the name of the channel. You will instantly "teleport" there. You can see everyone in every room at a glance without having to join them first.

3. The AFK System

Don't disconnect when you step away! Use the "Away" icon (the little clock) in the top toolbar. This mutes your mic and speakers and tags you as (Away). If you stay idle too long, the server will automatically move you to the AFK channel to keep things organized.

4. The "Poke" Function

Need someone's attention? Right-click their name and select "Poke Client." It sends a direct "Hey!" alert sound and a popup on their screen. It's the best way to wake someone up for a match.
